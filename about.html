About



Arthur Charles Edwards

Visual Web Designer | Front-End Enthusiast

Arthur joined the visual communication world with a background in fine arts. Classical painting, illustration, and digital media were the stepping stones to a career in web design. After finding an interest and focus in the IT industry, Arthur quickly gained experience with a fun internship at the University of Florida's Harn Museum of Art.
This accomplishment confirmed his desire to be a visual designer and soon after he acquired his first design position. Since these accomplishments, he has designed large web applications, learned Front-End development and Motion Media Design.

Arthur is a person that loves to challenge friends to old-school video games. Despite his love of the old-school, he loves the challenge of the new digital era. He focuses his efforts on visual web design, responsive frameworks and breaking into motion media design. With 7 years of experience, he is continuing to design web applications with a talented team at Florida Blue.

Please contact Arthur about contract design work, Full-time positions and or about collaborating on a project.

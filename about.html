About



Arthur Charles Edwards

Old School Gamer, New School Designer

Arthur is a designer that loves to challenge friends to old school video games. Despite his love of the old-school, he loves the challenge of the new digital era. He focuses his efforts on visual web design, responsive frameworks and breaking into motion media design. With 7 years of experience he is continuing to design web applications with a fun team at Florida Blue.
